"PRIMARY LINES" AWARD WINNING PSA ANIMATION (2022)

Directed and Animated by Hazel Ampon
Written by Hazel Ampon






DIRECTOR'S VISION


The concept was greatly inspired by optical illusions on lined paper, which eventually developed the idea of something or someone existing on the other side. The primary lined paper then became the symbol for basic education as it is used early in the elementary stage of education. I wanted to emphasize the reality that not every child has access to quality education, which deprives them of their growth and development. And so, using the lines as cage bars construes that the lack of basic education limits the child to the better life that they deserve.


ROTARY CIRCUIT COMPETITION


Primary Lines successfully represented the Lyceum of the Philippines University Manila,
winning third place overall in the Rotary PSA Festival on December 2022.

For more information, you may check out this article written by Seymour Sanchez.


LYCINEMA 2023


Primary Lines was awarded first prize in the
Public Service Announcement category of LYCINEMA 2023
