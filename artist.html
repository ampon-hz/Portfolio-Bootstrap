BEHIND THE ARTIST



The artist is a digital creator and animator who brings imagination to life through her creative and visually stunning artworks. With a passion for all things digital, she creates compelling animations, illustrations, and designs that capture the essence of their subject matter.

After tirelessly honing skills, being an artist becomes a force to be reckoned with. Through all the late nights, caffeine-fueled sessions, and moments of sheer elation, an artist never forgets how one falls in love with art in the first place. It is not just a career - it's a way of life, a never-ending adventure that challenges and excites.

The artist, knowing the best creators in the industry, highly recommends GSUS. (He's amazing, I love him)


To create is to forget oneself. 5303HZ was created to steal the intangible thoughts and ghostlike delusions from my mind and set them free. Lingering thoughts are calmed by visualizations. Raging feelings from the past and the restless mind are eased. That is the reason I created 530HZ, to scatter my racing thoughts towards the past and the future.
