CITY GOVERNMENT OF LIGAO

A rebranding project for the City of Ligao, Albay, as proposed by GUNITA

GUNITA X LIGAO:
THE CITY WHERE GOOD THINGS HAPPEN


(Brand Proposal Video by GSUS)




PURPOSE

This project aims to showcase the true nature of the multitude of attractions and delicacies in Ligao—developing their tourism growth to create a brand that will foster Ligao’s locations for travel and how people see Albay in another light through the area.


OPPORTUNITY

To give a new system and look for Ligao City's branding will greatly boost their identity and connections with their market.

The branding proposal will produce a marketable and modernized representation of the city utilized for internal and external use of communication with the audience. The presumed brand foundation for Ligao aims to achieve a cohesive visual identity to ensure consistent and effective connections for the city's ecotourism growth. Marketing materials and souvenirs will be designed as part of Ligao City's brand merchandising.

For Ligao, Albay to be recognized separately from just Albay, the rebranding can produce an impact for the community that will help recognize the city’s growth.

THE LOGOS OF LIGAO

Introducing the LIGAO logo and logo marks









WHAT MAKES IT TRULY LIGAO?

We have developed a logo design that truly makes Ligao recognizable. There are three (3) defining elements in this logo. The yellow curve that passes through the G and extends towards the letter A, which is a sunflower petal The other two elements are integrated with each other to represent the Mayon Volcano; and individually, the left stroke stands alone as the outline of Ligao city when seen from the map of the Philippines. The direction of this rebranding should give a natural, calming and refreshing feel to Ligao City as a brand. Should have a nice blend of cool colors with a single warm tone to balance everything out.
